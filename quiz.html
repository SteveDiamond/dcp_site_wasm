DCP Quiz

Note: The quiz functionality is not yet implemented in this client-side version. Please use the Analyzer to practice identifying DCP properties of expressions.

The quiz will test your understanding of DCP rules by presenting randomly generated expressions and asking you to identify their curvature and sign.

In the meantime, try analyzing these expressions in the Analyzer:

  • square(x) + square(y) - Should be convex
  • sqrt(x) + sqrt(y) - Should be concave
  • log(exp(x) + exp(y)) - Should be convex (log-sum-exp)
  • x*y - Should be unknown curvature (not DCP)
  • max(x, 0) - Should be convex (same as pos(x))
